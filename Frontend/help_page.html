Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?

You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "Order History" section. You'll find a tracking number and link to the carrier's website there. We'll also send you email updates as your order progresses.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items in original condition. Some items like clearance merchandise or personalized products may have different return conditions. Please check the product page for specific return information.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping (2 business days) and overnight shipping are also available at checkout. Delivery times may vary during holidays or peak seasons.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. Additional customs fees or taxes may apply depending on your country's regulations.

How can I change or cancel my order?

You can change or cancel your order within 1 hour of placing it by contacting our support team. After that time, your order enters our fulfillment process and changes may not be possible. In that case, you can return items after receipt.

Policies

Return Policy

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be in original condition with tags attached. Return shipping is free for defective or incorrect items. For other returns, customers are responsible for return shipping costs.

Shipping Policy

Orders placed before 2 PM EST are processed the same day. We offer free standard shipping on orders over $50. Express and overnight shipping options are available at checkout. International orders may be subject to customs fees.

Privacy Policy

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes. For more details, please see our full privacy policy.
